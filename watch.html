1. BLEACH

Bleach is a supernatural action anime that follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who gains the powers of a Soul Reaper after an unexpected encounter with Rukia Kuchiki. He begins protecting humans from evil spirits known as Hollows while uncovering mysteries within the Soul Society. The anime is famous for its sword fights, transformations, and iconic characters.

2. BLUE BOX

Blue Box is a sports and romance anime that tells the story of Taiki Inomata, a badminton player who admires basketball star Chinatsu Kano. As their lives slowly become closer, the anime explores school life, dreams, sports, and emotional relationships in a wholesome and realistic way.

3. DEATH NOTE

Death Note is a psychological thriller anime centered around Light Yagami, a genius student who discovers a mysterious notebook capable of killing anyone whose name is written in it. As he tries to create a new world free from crime, a brilliant detective named L begins a dangerous battle of intelligence against him.

4. ATTACK ON TITAN

Attack On Titan is a dark fantasy anime set in a world where humanity lives behind giant walls to survive from terrifying Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they uncover shocking truths about their world through intense battles, emotional sacrifices, and unforgettable plot twists.

5. GINTAMA

Gintama is a comedy and action anime that follows Gintoki Sakata, a lazy samurai living in an alternate version of Japan invaded by aliens. Known for its hilarious humor, parodies, emotional arcs, and sudden serious fights, the anime perfectly balances comedy and action.

6. VINLAND SAGA

Vinland Saga is a historical action anime inspired by Viking history. It follows Thorfinn, a young warrior seeking revenge for his father's death while struggling to understand the true meaning of violence, war, and freedom.

7. YOUR LIE IN APRIL

Your Lie in April is a music and drama anime about Kousei Arima, a piano prodigy who loses his passion for music after personal tragedy. His life changes when he meets Kaori Miyazono, a cheerful violinist who inspires him to rediscover music and life itself.

8. ONE PUNCH MAN

One Punch Man is a superhero comedy anime about Saitama, a hero so powerful that he can defeat any enemy with a single punch. Despite his incredible strength, he struggles with boredom and searches for a true challenge.

9. HAIKYUU!!

Haikyuu!! is a sports anime focused on volleyball and teamwork. The story follows Shoyo Hinata, a short but energetic player determined to become a great volleyball athlete despite his height. The anime is loved for its hype matches, character growth, and motivational energy.

10. STEINS;GATE

Steins;Gate is a sci-fi thriller anime about a group of friends who accidentally discover a way to send messages through time. As their experiments begin affecting reality, they become trapped in dangerous timelines filled with mystery, suspense, and emotional moments.

11. SPY X FAMILY

Spy X Family is a comedy and slice-of-life anime about a spy who must create a fake family to complete an important mission. However, he does not realize that his adopted daughter is a telepath and his wife is an assassin. The anime mixes wholesome family moments with action and comedy.

12. HORIMIYA

Horimiya is a romantic comedy anime that follows the relationship between Kyoko Hori and Izumi Miyamura, two students with completely different personalities inside and outside school. The anime focuses on friendship, romance, and everyday teenage life in a wholesome and emotional way.

13. PARASYTE

Parasyte is a horror and psychological anime where mysterious alien parasites invade Earth and take control of human bodies. The story follows Shinichi Izumi, whose right hand becomes infected by a parasite named Migi, leading to intense battles and questions about humanity and survival.
